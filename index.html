About Me

Bet you don't get many timelines on your applications

  • 2009-Present

    The First Real Challenge

    Early on in his life Mr. Davis became fascinated with the outdoors, history, teamwork. He wasn't interested in the local sports, so he decided to make his own team that could help make himself and others feel a sense of belonging. This was when he formed the Bolivar/Fairplay/North Springfield chapter of Dagorhir, and as administrator, handled all memberships, waivers of liability, an notarization of documents. Dagorhir is a combat sport similar to fencing, with lots of camping and historical/fantasy costuming and crafting. At it's height, this chapter of Dagorhir maintained a regular 30+ members and twice weekly events.

  • January 2011

    Leadership and Education

    Mr. Davis achieved the rank of Second Lieutenant in the local Air Force JROTC. He showed commitment and leadership in parades, drill, and fundraising events while being promoted to Flight Commander in a mere 6 month period.

  • 2013-2014

    Not All Risks Are Rewarded...

    Mr. Davis met his wife in late 2012, but felt his job in retail wouldn't provide enough for them in the long term. Everyone had always pressured him to persue additonal education, and, not knowing what else to do, Mr. Davis enrolled in the Art Institute of Pittsburgh. After a year and a half of struggling with the online format and lack of direction, Mr. Davis eventually had no choice but to remove himself from enrollment. Things looked pretty bad for him financially, as he couldn't afford the payments without the degree. He Wondered if he could ever recover from this tragedy.

  • July 2020

    ...But Persistence is.

    After working in backbreaking and demoralizing positions of labor, and staying strong through the toughest times, Mr. Davis eventually found the means to make it back into school, this time focusing on Computer Science, a field he has always had a talent with. It wasn't long before Mr. Davis was working on big projects, designing systems of cellular automata in object oriented design.

  • THE FUTURE

    A bright light at the end of a decade-long tunnel.

    With Mr. Davis' new skills and sense of adventure, he's capable of anything. One day he intends to start his own company, but desires greatly to enter the field of Ecommerce and really show what he's got in the near future.

Forms Map Generator

Surprisingly complex to do in such a simple program.

The dream team developed a cellular automaton algorithm that took a generated grid of tiles and, based on the identity of the tiles, mutated the map to better emulate real geography. The initial map would be random tiles, and the iterations of Automata would slowly form isolated tiles of water into lakes, grass into fields, and even structures. Later versions were updated to initialize the generation with Perlin Noise.

  • Client: OTC Project
  • Category: Programming
